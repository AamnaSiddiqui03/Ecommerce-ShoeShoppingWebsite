About Us

Our Mission

Aamna, Amit, Sidharth, and Bhuvana came together with a passion for footwear and a vision to revolutionize the online shopping experience for men's shoes. Thus, they founded 'SoleStride,' an e-commerce website dedicated to making shoe shopping easy and convenient for customers. As the pandemic brought about significant changes in people's lifestyles, they recognized the increasing importance of technology in shaping consumer habits. In response to the changing times, SoleStride embraced innovative approaches, offering a diverse range of men's footwear, including sport shoes, sandals, and formal shoes. What set them apart from other online shoe stores was their unique 'Try Before You Pay' principle, which allowed customers to make their first purchase and then pay later. This approach gained immense popularity and trust among customers, fostering loyalty and repeat business. Within just two weeks of launching their platform, SoleStride achieved a remarkable milestone of 500 customer purchases, demonstrating the strong demand for their products and customer-friendly policies. Their monthly sales consistently exceeded 5 lakhs, setting the stage for even more ambitious growth in the future. With a clear vision and unwavering dedication, the team at SoleStride is now aiming to hit the significant milestone of achieving annual sales of 10 crore or more. The company's founders dream of turning SoleStride into a Unicorn Company within a decade, symbolizing their aspiration for remarkable success and influence in the footwear industry. As they move forward, SoleStride continues to focus on providing high-quality footwear, exceptional customer service, and staying at the forefront of emerging tech trends. They believe that by staying true to their core values and delivering exceptional value to their customers, they can create a lasting impact in the world of men's shoes and beyond
